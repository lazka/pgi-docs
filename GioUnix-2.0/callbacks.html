Details¶

GioUnix.DesktopAppLaunchCallback(appinfo, pid, *user_data)¶
Parameters:

  • appinfo (Gio.DesktopAppInfo) – a GioUnix.DesktopAppInfo

  • pid (int) – Process identifier

  • user_data (object or None) – User data

During invocation, GioUnix.DesktopAppInfo.launch_uris_as_manager() may create one or more child processes. This callback is invoked once for each, providing the process ID.
