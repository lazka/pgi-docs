  • callback (Gio.AsyncReadyCallback or None) – A Gio.AsyncReadyCallback to call when the request is satisfied or None.

Asynchronously invokes the ‘Hello() [gdbus-method-org-gnome-DisplayManager-WorkerManager.Hello]’ D-Bus method on self. When the operation is finished, callback will be invoked in the thread-default main loop of the thread you are calling this method from.
