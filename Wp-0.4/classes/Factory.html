Wp.FactoryClass

The Wp.Factory class allows accessing the properties and methods of PipeWire Factory objects (struct pw_factory). A Wp.Factory is constructed internally by wireplumber, when the pipewire constructed factory objects are reported in by PipeWire registry and it is made available for wireplumber clients through the Wp.ObjectManager API.

New in version 0.4.5.
