WebKit.DeviceInfoPermissionRequestClass

A permission request for accessing user’s audio/video devices.

WebKit.UserMediaPermissionRequest represents a request for permission to whether WebKit should be allowed to access the user’s devices information when requested through the enumerateDevices API.

When a WebKit.DeviceInfoPermissionRequest is not handled by the user, it is denied by default.

New in version 2.24.
