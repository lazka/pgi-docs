WebKit.NavigationPolicyDecisionClass

A policy decision for navigation actions.

WebKit.NavigationPolicyDecision represents a policy decision for events associated with navigations. If the value of WebKit.NavigationPolicyDecision :mouse-button is not 0, then the navigation was triggered by a mouse event.
