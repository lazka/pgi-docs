GtkSource.CompletionSnippetsClass

A [iface`CompletionProvider`] for the completion of snippets.

The GtkSourceCompletionSnippets is an example of an implementation of the [iface`CompletionProvider`] interface. The proposals are snippets registered with the [class`SnippetManager`].
