set_style(style)¶
Parameters:style (Mx.Style) – a Mx.Style

Sets style as the new Mx.Style to be used by self.

The Mx.Stylable will take ownership of the passed Mx.Style.

After the Mx.Style has been set, the Mx.Stylable ::style-set signal will be emitted.

style_changed(flags)¶
Parameters:flags (Mx.StyleChangedFlags) – flags that control the style changing

Emit the “style-changed” signal on self to notify it that one or more of the style properties has changed.

If self is a Clutter.Container then the “style-changed” notification is propagated to it’s children, since their style may depend on one or more properties of the parent.
