classmethod blobs(old_blob, old_as_path, new_blob, new_as_path, diff_options, file_cb, binary_cb, hunk_cb, line_cb, *user_data)¶
Parameters

  • old_blob (Ggit.Blob or None) – a Ggit.Blob to diff from.

  • old_as_path (str or None) – treat old_blob as if it had this filename, or None,

  • new_blob (Ggit.Blob or None) – a Ggit.Blob to diff to.

  • new_as_path (str or None) – treat new_blob as if it had this filename, or None,

  • diff_options (Ggit.DiffOptions or None) – a Ggit.DiffOptions, or None.

  • file_cb (Ggit.DiffFileCallback or None) – a Ggit.DiffFileCallback.

  • binary_cb (Ggit.DiffBinaryCallback or None) – a Ggit.DiffBinaryCallback.

  • hunk_cb (Ggit.DiffHunkCallback or None) – a Ggit.DiffHunkCallback.

  • line_cb (Ggit.DiffLineCallback or None) – a Ggit.DiffLineCallback.

  • user_data (object or None) – callback user data.

Raises

GLib.Error

Iterates over the diff calling file_cb, binary_cb, hunk_cb and line_cb.

The Ggit.DiffFile mode always be 0, path will be None and when a blob is None the oid will be 0.

If diff_options is None then the defaults specified in Ggit.DiffOptions.new() are used.
