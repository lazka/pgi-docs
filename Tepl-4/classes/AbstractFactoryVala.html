set_singleton_vala()¶

Like Tepl.AbstractFactory.set_singleton(), but with (transfer none) for the self parameter.

Apparently Vala doesn’t support (transfer full) for the self parameter, resulting to a double unref if Tepl.AbstractFactory.set_singleton() is called in Vala…

New in version 4.0.
