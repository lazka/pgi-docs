set_spell_checker(spell_checker)¶
Parameters:

spell_checker (Gspell.Checker or None) – a Gspell.Checker, or None to unset the spell checker.

Sets a Gspell.Checker to a Gspell.EntryBuffer. The self will own a reference to spell_checker, so you can release your reference to spell_checker if you no longer need it.

New in version 1.4.
